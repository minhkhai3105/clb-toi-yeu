Fernando Torres

Fernando José Torres Sanz is a Spanish football manager and former player who played as a striker. He is the current manager of Atlético Madrid B. Due to his consistent goalscoring as a young player, Torres came to be nicknamed El Niño, which stuck with him throughout his career.


Born: March 20, 1984 (age 40 years), Municipality of Fuenlabrada, Spain

Number: 9 (Liverpool F.C. / Forward), 9 (AC Milan / Forward)

Position: Forward Height: 1.86 m Spouse: Olalla Domínguez Liste (m. 2009)

Parents: José Torres, Flori Sanz Torres Siblings: Israel Torres, Maria Paz Torres
