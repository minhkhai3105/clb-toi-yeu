Chelsea vs Barca Semi-Final C1 2012

Why is the Torres goal againt Barcelona in 2012 so glorified?


It was an epic moment. Difficult to explain if you weren’t a fan at the time, but I’ll try: Chelsea were massive underdogs. Over the two matches they were getting absolutely bombarded left and right by Barça (one of the best teams ever) but still by some miracle holding on to the advantage they had accumulated. In that match specifically it was 10v11. Everyone was waiting for the moment Barça were finally going to break through, but then in the final minutes there’s a sudden opening, Chelsea sees the chance for a counter attack.
Torres goes the length of the field, dribbles the goalkeeper, and deposits the ball in the net to seal the deal. Moreover, Torres had been extremely underwhelming for Chelsea up to that point, and that goal reminded the world of the Torres of old. It was a very impactful moment at the time, as a neutral it stayed with me to this day.
